Where and When

The ceremony will be held on Saturday, January 12, 2015 at Manalia Tower Floor 24 Room 256, Anfix Street ST01. It will start at Five O'Clock in the evenings. A celebration with dinner and dancing will follow at Forine Restaurant, Rose Street ST08. Check the map to get the direction. We have arranged accommodations at a nearby comfort inn for guests who are making a bit of a trip. Check for accomodations.
